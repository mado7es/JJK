Background

Yuji Itadori is the main protagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen. He was a regular high school student until he encountered a cursed object—Sukuna's finger—and became its vessel to save his friends.

Despite his situation, Yuji maintains an optimistic personality and strong moral compass, determined to help others and consume all of Sukuna's fingers to eliminate the King of Curses.

Abilities

  • Superhuman Physicality: Extraordinary strength, speed, and durability
  • Divergent Fist: Delayed cursed energy impact
  • Black Flash: Spatial distortion when cursed energy connects within 0.000001 seconds
  • Sukuna's Techniques: Access to some of Sukuna's abilities

Role in Story

Yuji is a first-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High under the guidance of Satoru Gojo. His mission is to locate and consume all 20 of Sukuna's fingers to eliminate the curse forever.

He forms close bonds with classmates Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki, and despite the danger he poses as Sukuna's vessel, he's determined to use his power to save people.

Personality

Yuji is characterized by his kindness, empathy, and strong sense of justice. He believes in helping others and values all human life, which often puts him at odds with the jujutsu world's harsh realities.

Despite the immense burden of hosting Sukuna, Yuji remains surprisingly optimistic and determined to live a life where he can save as many people as possible.

Background

Satoru Gojo is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and widely recognized as the strongest in the world. He comes from the prestigious Gojo Clan and is the first person to inherit both the Limitless technique and the Six Eyes in centuries.

He works as a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High where he mentors the next generation of sorcerers, including Yuji Itadori, Megumi Fushiguro, and Nobara Kugisaki.

Abilities

  • Limitless: Manipulation of space at an atomic level
  • Six Eyes: Extraordinary perception and cursed energy efficiency
  • Infinity: Automatic defense that slows approaching objects infinitely
  • Hollow Purple: Imaginary mass that erases matter from existence
  • Domain Expansion: Unlimited Void: Overwhelms targets with infinite information

Role in Story

Gojo serves as a mentor to the protagonists and is a major force for change in the jujutsu world. He aims to reform the corrupt jujutsu society and nurture strong allies who can stand beside him.

His immense power maintains balance in the world, preventing catastrophic curses from overwhelming humanity. His sealing during the Shibuya Incident creates a major turning point in the series.

Personality

Gojo is confident, playful, and often irreverent, masking a more serious and calculating nature. He cares deeply for his students and friends but maintains emotional distance due to his overwhelming power.

Despite his casual demeanor, he's a strategic thinker committed to creating a better jujutsu world, even if it means going against traditional authorities.

Background

Toji Fushiguro, born Toji Zenin, is a former member of the Zenin Clan who left due to his lack of cursed energy. He possesses a Heavenly Restriction that grants him superhuman physical abilities in exchange for no cursed energy.

He worked as a mercenary and assassin, earning the title "Sorcerer Killer" for his ability to defeat even special grade sorcerers. He's the father of Megumi Fushiguro.

Abilities

  • Heavenly Restriction: Superhuman strength, speed, and senses without cursed energy
  • Cursed Energy Invisibility: Undetectable to sorcerers and curses due to zero cursed energy
  • Cursed Tool Mastery: Expert use of various cursed tools and weapons
  • Inventory Curse: Stores weapons in a curse's stomach for instant retrieval
  • Split Soul Katana: Ignores durability by targeting the soul directly

Role in Story

Toji plays a crucial role in Gojo's past, being the only person to ever defeat him (before Gojo's awakening to Reverse Cursed Technique). His actions directly influence the events of the Hidden Inventory arc.

His legacy continues through his son Megumi, whom he sold to the Zenin clan before his death. Toji's existence challenges the jujutsu world's belief that cursed energy is necessary for power.

Personality

Toji is cynical, pragmatic, and driven by material needs due to his impoverished upbringing. He rejects the jujutsu world that rejected him, viewing sorcerers with contempt.

Despite his cold exterior, he shows unexpected care for Megumi, ensuring his son would have a better life than he did, even if through unconventional means.

Background

Ryomen Sukuna is the undisputed King of Curses, a legendary figure from the Heian Era who was so powerful that even after death, his remains persisted as 20 indestructible cursed objects (fingers).

In the present day, he becomes incarnated within Yuji Itadori after Yuji consumes one of his fingers. Sukuna represents the pinnacle of cursed energy mastery and pure malevolence.

Abilities

  • Cleave and Dismantle: Slashing techniques that adapt to target's toughness or cut everything in range
  • Fire Arrow: Devastating fire-based attack revealed during Jogo's battle
  • Domain Expansion: Malevolent Shrine: 200-meter barrierless domain with guaranteed hits
  • Reverse Cursed Technique: Can heal himself and others
  • Unknown Cursed Techniques: Sukuna possesses additional unrevealed techniques

Role in Story

Sukuna serves as the primary antagonist of the series, though he operates through Yuji as his vessel. He seeks to regain his full power by having Yuji consume all his fingers.

He occasionally takes control of Yuji's body under specific conditions, causing catastrophic damage. His ultimate goals remain mysterious but involve reshaping the world according to his desires.

Personality

Sukuna is arrogant, cruel, and self-indulgent, viewing others as inferior beings to be used or destroyed according to his whims. He follows no moral code beyond his own pleasure and interests.

Despite his malevolence, he respects strength and skill, occasionally showing interest in powerful opponents like Gojo. He maintains a complex relationship with Yuji, often mocking his host's morality while occasionally assisting him.
